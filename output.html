* **Afternoon Snack (4:00 PM):** Greek yogurt (200g, full-fat) with granola (50g) and blueberries (80g).
    * Protein: 20g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 65g

* **Dinner (7:00 PM):** Lean ground turkey (160g) with brown rice (90g dry weight), steamed sweet potato (200g), and broccoli (150g) with olive oil (12g).
